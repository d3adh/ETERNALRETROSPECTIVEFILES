SITE PURPOSE

━━━━━━━━━▼━━━━━━━━━

The purpose of this site is as its name suggests: Introspection. Self-reflection. We can never truly grow without reflecting upon ourselves, least we lose track of our own progress and goals. I also use this site as a sort of yearly catalogue, adding a page once a year each December with a recap of the year. I also have a journal on the site, updated whenever i feel the need.
